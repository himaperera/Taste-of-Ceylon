Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our recipes and services

How can I adapt Sri Lankan recipes for dietary restrictions?

Many Sri Lankan recipes can be adapted for various dietary needs. For gluten-free options, replace wheat flour with rice flour or chickpea flour. For vegan dishes, substitute coconut milk for dairy and use plant-based proteins. Our chefs can provide specific modifications for any dietary requirement.

Where can I find authentic Sri Lankan ingredients?

Many Sri Lankan spices and ingredients are now available in international grocery stores or online specialty shops. For hard-to-find items, we often suggest suitable substitutes in our recipes. You can also contact us directly for guidance on sourcing authentic ingredients.

Can I request a specific recipe from a particular region of Sri Lanka?

Absolutely! We welcome recipe requests and are always happy to research and share regional specialties. Contact us with your request, and our chefs will work on creating an authentic version for you.

Do you offer cooking classes or workshops?

Yes, we regularly host cooking classes both in-person at our Colombo studio and online. Our classes range from beginner-friendly introductions to Sri Lankan cooking to advanced workshops on specific techniques. Check our website or contact us for the current schedule.
