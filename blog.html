Sri Lankan New Year Traditions: The Foods That Celebrate Spring

Explore the culinary traditions of Sri Lanka's Sinhala and Tamil New Year. From kiribath (milk rice) to kavum (oil cakes), discover the symbolic foods that mark this important cultural celebration. Learn about the auspicious times for preparing and consuming these traditional dishes and how families across the island welcome the new year with feasts that honor both Buddhist and Hindu traditions.

Beyond the Curry: Exploring Sri Lanka's Rich Vegetarian Heritage

While Sri Lankan cuisine is famous for its meat and seafood dishes, the island has a vibrant vegetarian tradition rooted in Buddhist and Hindu practices. Discover delicious plant-based dishes that showcase the diversity of local produce.
